AA note. Navigation — floating Liquid Glass tab bar

iOS-26 style floating bar carrying four items: Home, Shows, Up Next, Search (tapping Search takes the bar over as a search field; Settings moved to a top-right gear). Blurred --tabbar-glass fill, --tabbar-hairline edge, --tabbar-shadow lift, inactive --tabbar-icon, active --accent. Shown over bright artwork to prove label contrast.

AA over bright content — worst case measured. Labels are small text (.62rem/600), so the bar is 4.5:1. Dark: rgba(14,11,18,.94) stays dark over any backdrop; worst case (over white) inactive --tabbar-icon #8B8291 = 4.69:1, active coral = 6.10:1; over the app's saturated artwork both climb higher. Light: the near-white fill was raised to rgba(250,248,252,.96) so saturated art no longer bleeds through and drops the bar. Over the worst backdrop (grape/pink album art) inactive #6C6C70 = 4.74:1 and active #CA340F = 4.74:1; over plain surface/white ≈4.95–4.97. All ≥ 4.5:1 small-text AA in both themes, over both bright and dark backdrops.
